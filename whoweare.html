Red Fox Tech Consulting is Marist University’s New student governed Technology consulting society.

Our Mission

Our work is free and best-effort, with clear scope and clean handoff, so clients get something usable, and our members build relevant skills. We help local organizations solve real problems with practical skills, impactful solutions, workflow automation, vibrant dashboards, and better system designs.

We exist to cultivate community for graduate students to build intellectual maturity, refine and practice their skills, and gain the professional confidence to ensure their career readiness. Through collaboration, mentorship, and experiential opportunities, we support holistic development that extends beyond the classroom.

Our Story

Red Fox Tech Consulting was created to give students a real consulting environment inside campus, not just theory. The goal is simple: build professional skills through meaningful work, with a strong standard for quality and accountability.

As we grow, we’ll expand projects, workshops, and partnerships with Marist departments and local organizations, so members graduate with real experience and real results.
